About the Artist

I dream of Painting and then I paint my dream.
— Vincent Van Gogh.

I am Udita Dasgupta, a Bengali girl brought up for the majority of my life in Dubai. I am a chemical engineering student with a passion for glass painting and poetry. The idea of a piece of my art brightening someone's room fascinates me.

A painting can say countless things that can't be expressed in words. It can mean different things to every eye that is laid upon it.
